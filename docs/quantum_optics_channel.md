# Quantum Optics Channel Documentation

## Overview

Simulates quantum optics experiments (SPDC, HOM) with scalar field modifications.

## Methodology

- **SPDC**: Biphoton correlation modifications
- **HOM**: Interference visibility modifications
- **Note**: All simulations, no human influence

## Implementation

- Modules: `code/inference/quantum_optics/spdc_simulation.py`, `hom_interference.py`
- Data: `data/constraints/quantum_optics/`

## Sensitivity

- Optical frequency range: m_c ~ 10⁻³ - 10⁻¹ eV
